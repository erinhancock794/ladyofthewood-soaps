Our Story

Welcome to AZ Lady of the Wood, where every bar of soap is a labor of love. I create each batch by hand using the traditional cold process method, ensuring that every beneficial property of my carefully selected ingredients is preserved.

My journey began with a simple desire: to create soap that's truly good for your skin. No harsh chemicals, no synthetic fragrances, no questionable ingredients. Just pure, nourishing soap that leaves your skin feeling soft, clean, and cared for.

Every soap is made in small batches, allowing me to maintain the highest quality standards while infusing each bar with intention and care.
