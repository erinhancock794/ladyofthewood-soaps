Frequently Asked Questions

What is cold process soap?

Cold process soap is made by mixing oils with lye (sodium hydroxide) without external heat. This traditional method preserves the natural glycerin and beneficial properties of the oils, resulting in a gentler, more moisturizing bar.

Are your soaps safe for sensitive skin?

Yes! Our soaps are free of harsh chemicals, synthetic fragrances, and artificial preservatives. We use only skin-safe dyes and natural ingredients. However, always check the ingredient list for potential allergens.

How long do your soaps last?

With proper care (keeping them dry between uses on a draining soap dish), each bar typically lasts 4-6 weeks with daily use. The shelf life of unused bars is about 1 year when stored in a cool, dry place.

Do you offer custom orders?

Absolutely! We love creating custom soaps for special occasions like weddings, baby showers, or corporate gifts. Contact us with your ideas and we'll work together to create something unique.
